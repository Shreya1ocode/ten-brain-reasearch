Neuphony EXG Synapse Product Information

Neuphony EXG Synapse

DIY HCI/BCI & Robotics Kit for beginners with biopotential signal compatibility, covering ECG, EEG, EOG, and EMG.

Product Description

Dive into DIY neuroscience with EXG Synapse Kits! Perfect for students and innovators, effortlessly capture EMG, ECG, EEG, and EOG signals. Explore the brain and muscles—it’s hands-on and exciting. Neuphony Synapse has comprehensive biopotential signal compatibility, covering ECG, EEG, EOG, and EMG, ensuring a versatile solution for various physiological monitoring applications. It seamlessly pairs with any MCU featuring ADC, expanding compatibility across platforms like Arduino, ESP32, STM32, and more. Enjoy flexibility with an optional bypass of the bandpass filter allowing tailored signal output for diverse analysis.

Specification Details
Input Voltage 3.3V
Input Impedance 209 Ω
Compatible Hardware Any ADC input
Biopotentials ECG, EMG, EOG, or EEG (configurable bandpass) | By default configured for a bandwidth of 1.6Hz to 47Hz and Gain 50
No. of Channels 1
Electrodes 3
