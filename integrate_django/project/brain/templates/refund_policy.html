REFUND POLICY

1. TERMS OF REFUND

The following requirements must be met in order for the company to provide the user a refund within 15–21 business days:

  1. if the user want to return the product because they are unhappy with it.
  2. if the user requested to cancel the order for the product within 24 hours after placing it.
    • If the delivered product is damaged, differs from what was requested, or the box is opened prior to delivery, the client will receive a replacement.
  3. Online payment methods will either issue a store credit or a refund to the original account in which the purchase was made.
  4. In the event of a complete policy return, the company will inspect the products and conduct a quality check. Within 15–21 business days following the inspection and quality verification, the money return will be completed.
