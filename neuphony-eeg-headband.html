Ten Brain Research Product Information

Ten Brain Research Headband

6 channel EEG headset for brain training and neurofeedback at home

Product Description

Neuphony Headband is a neurofeedback-based wearable brain device (EEG Headband) that captures key mind parameters and allows users to know their stress and external focus scores. The device has 8 sensors - 6 signal and 2 reference distributed in the brain's parietal, frontal & temporal lobes. These are the polymer sensors that are conducive in nature. Cognitive States To Measure:

    1.Stress Vs Calm
    2.Mood Analysis
    3.External Focus Vs Distractions
    4.PDR (Posterior Dominant Rhythm)

Parameter Value
Sensor Count 6
Resolution 24 bits per channel
Dynamic Range 375 mV (pp)
Motion Sensor 3x accelerometer
Battery Life Up to 8 hours
Sample Rate 250 SPS
Sensor Locations Fp1, Fp2, F3, F4, Fz, T3, T4, Pz
LSB Resolution 22.35 nVolts (24 bits mode)
Sensor Technology Dry sensors: polycarbonate conductive passive electrodes with Ag/AgCl coating
Sensor Technology CMS/DRL references at T3/T4
Frequency Response 1 45 Hz
Connectivity Bluetooth Low Energy 4.2, USB Type-C (with Extender)
Supported Platforms Windows 10 (64-bit) v1607+, Mac OSX 10.13 and higher, iOS 11 or above, Android 5.0 Lollipop or above
